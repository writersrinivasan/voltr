The Problem

Rural students are
structurally disadvantaged

🚫

No Industry Access

Rural college students rarely meet working professionals. Career guidance comes from textbooks, not experience.

🗣️

Communication Gap

Poor English fluency and lack of soft-skills coaching leaves students unprepared for job interviews and corporate environments.

💻

Tech Blindspot

Zero exposure to AI, cloud computing, and modern software tools — skills that are now table-stakes in any technology career.

63%of rural graduates unemployed within 6 months of degree
4%of rural students have ever spoken to an industry professional
₹0average career counselling budget in rural colleges
Our Solution

VOLTR is the
bridge they need

A structured, volunteer-driven mentoring ecosystem — powered by AI — that connects professionals directly with rural students through a trusted, managed platform.

🙋

Volunteer Registration Portal

Professionals register in 5 minutes. We verify, onboard, and match them to students who need their specific expertise.

⚙️

Admin Management Platform

Full review workflow, communications center, audit logs, and reporting for program coordinators.

🤝

Mentor–Student Matching

AI-powered matching based on skills, goals, schedule, and language — so every session has maximum impact.

📊

Impact Measurement

Pre/post assessments, session tracking, and a live impact dashboard for donors and institutional partners.

Product — Phase 1 MVP

Everything needed to
launch and scale volunteer intake

Volunteer Portal

  • 5-step multi-page registration wizard
  • Email verification and account confirmation
  • Volunteer dashboard with status tracking
  • Profile editor — update skills and availability
  • Real-time application status visibility
  • Notification preferences (email / SMS)

Admin Portal

  • KPI dashboard with growth charts
  • Review queue with approve / reject / flag actions
  • Volunteer search and filter (50+ parameters)
  • Email communication center with audience targeting
  • Full audit log of all admin actions
  • Role management (Super Admin, Admin, Reviewer)
  • CSV / PDF data export

built to scale
